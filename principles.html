The Mission Viejo Chess Club has Established a club ranking system for its members that will allow you to progress from pawn up to king by being active and attending club events either online, or in person, and showing chess knowledge. Below are the ranks and required knowledge for each rank.

Opening Principles

  1. Open with a center pawn until you reach expert level
  2. Develop all pieces before moving any piece twice, if possible. Get the whole team into the game.
  3. Develop your knights before bishops.
  4. Castle as soon as possible, preferably before move 10 and on the king side.
  5. Make as few pawn moves as possible in the opening.
  6. Develop pieces with threats if you can, or to block a threat. Try to do double duty.
  7. Don't bring out your queen to early.
  8. Always play to gain control of the center.
  9. Try to maintain at least one pawn in the center.
  10. Don't sacrifice without a clear and adequate reason.
