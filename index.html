Mission Viejo Chess Club

The Mission Viejo Chess Club is open to anyone interested in playing chess in South Orange County as well as playing online at chess.com. We currently have over 40 members ranging from beginners to strong intermediate level players.

The Mission Viejo Chess Club is open to anyone who would like to play casual chess, meet new friends, and have fun.

We currently play online matches against other clubs, have club tournaments, and occasional Zoom chess lessons for members. We have matches and tournaments suited for members of all skill levels.
